About Dizzy

DJ Dizzy is a passionate Egyptian DJ whose journey began far from home, launching his career in Russia before taking his sound across more than eight countries. Known for his distinctive style, DJ Dizzy blends popular, high-energy tracks with hypnotic underground techno and tech-house, creating sets that are both accessible and deeply immersive. His ability to bridge mainstream appeal with underground depth sets him apart, allowing him to connect with diverse crowds while maintaining a strong, authentic electronic identity. Driven by rhythm, culture, and constant evolution, DJ Dizzy continues to push boundaries on dance floors around the world.
